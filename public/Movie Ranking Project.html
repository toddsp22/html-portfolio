The Best Movies According to Todd

The Fiddler on the roof.

The Fellowship of the Ring

This movie came at a particulary hard time for me. The music of Enya lifted me so much, I watched the movie 7 times just to hear the song "May It Be." Plus Tolkien is my favorite author.
